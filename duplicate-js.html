Introducing new packages for DirectoryEngine and FreelanceEngine

Updated on January 7, 2016 in Customizing
Unfollow Follow
Dakachi 1 0 on May 25, 2015

Today, we are happy to introduce the latest packages applied for the two premium themes: DirectoryEngine and FreelanceEngine.

Similar to ClassifiedEngine and JobEngine packages, the new ones offer you themes and extensions with more than 20% discount on the products.

Get all the extensions at a good price, you can then extend your themes with even more features.

Sounds great, right? And it’s even more, if you’ve already purchased the themes, you can easily upgrade your current plan to a better solution.

Here’s the details:

DirectoryEngine:

  • DirectoryEngine Plus Package: get the theme and 6 great extensions with just $169 – 15% off of the original price.
  • DirectoryEngine Premium Package: 25% discount offered – only $199 for the themes plus all the extensions valued up to $259.
  • DirectoryEngine Standard Upgrade: update the current plan to the Plus Package with $80.
  • DirectoryEngine Full Upgrade: $110 more to get all the extensions

FreelanceEngine:

  • FreelanceEngine Plus Package: get the theme and 6 great extensions with just $159 – 15% off of the original price.
  • FreelanceEngine Premium Package: 25% discount offered – only $189 for the themes plus all the extensions valued up to $249.
  • FreelanceEngine Standard Upgrade: update to the Plus Package with $70.
  • FreelanceEngine Full Upgrade: $100 more to get all the extensions.


If you have any further questions about the upgrade policy, feel free to leave us a message at contact@enginethemes.com.What are you waiting for? Check out now and get the good deals!
